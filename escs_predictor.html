Electronic Stopping Cross-Section Predictor in-browser demo

Pick an ion, a target, and an energy — get the predicted electronic stopping S(E) with an approximate uncertainty band.

—
S  [dataset units, MeV/(mg/cm²)-scaled]
This is a lightweight in-browser model (compact gradient boosting, ~16.1% MAPE) embedded for instant demonstration. The full calibrated model (NGBoost, ~8.8% MAPE, with proper 90% credible intervals) and all data are in the repository: github.com/hussein-alnaffakh1984/escs-ml · DOI 10.5281/zenodo.20526130. The shaded band is an approximate ± based on the model’s energy-regime MAPE, not a calibrated posterior.

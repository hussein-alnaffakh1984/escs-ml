Bethe = textbook electronic stopping (bare charge, no shell/effective-charge corrections): accurate at high energy, diverges at low energy. Units verified against experiment (~2.5% at high-energy protons). These browser tools use a lightweight model (~16% MAPE); the full calibrated NGBoost model (~8.8%, 90% intervals) and all data: github.com/hussein-alnaffakh1984/escs-ml · DOI 10.5281/zenodo.20526130.
